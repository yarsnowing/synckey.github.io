iterm2 clone session

vim ~/.ssh/config

贴入如下内容:

Host *
    ServerAliveInterval 30
    ControlMaster auto
    ControlPath ~/.ssh/master-%r@%h:%p
    ControlPersist yes

退出iterm2，重新登录即可。
